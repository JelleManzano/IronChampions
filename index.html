You have been strandled in the middle of an unknown planet by your superiors to prove them your worth!

Select your IronChampion to start the game.

Use WASD to move your champion.

Use the SpaceBar to shoot an erradicate the aliens!

Pick up health packs to add +3 to your HP!

Be careful with the nurglings, they add 10 seconds to your time!

And be even more careful with the orcs as they will deal x5 times the damage to you as a regular demon!

Survive for 2 minutes to prove your worth to your superiors!
